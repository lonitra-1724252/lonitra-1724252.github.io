What is Boombi?

Have you ever had a song stuck in your head that you want to listen, but just don't know what it's called? Now there's Boombi! Boombi is a smart headphone that can read your brainwaves.

Here's how it works. After activation, start thinking of a song you want to listen to. You can either think of a tune, song lyrics, or the title with or without the artist, and Boombi will use that information to find the song you are thinking of and play it for you.

When will it be released?

Boombi is projected to be released in 2030. The project started in 2020 and we are working vigorously to ensure the utmost best quality and safety.
